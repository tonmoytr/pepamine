how to buy

create a wallet

Download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.io

get some SOL

You will need SOL in your wallet to switch to PEPAMINE. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Go to Raydium.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the PEPAMINE token address into Raydium, select PEPAMINE, and confirm. When phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap

Switch SOL for $PEPAMINE. Welcome to the  PEPAMINE family!
